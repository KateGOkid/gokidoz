About

Boston kids, all year round

GOkidOZ exists because parents shouldn't spend Sunday evenings frantically googling "what to do with the kids next week."

Our story

Built by one very determined Boston mom

Picture a Boston mother of two, both kids with gloriously different interests (one wants robots, the other wants ribbons), and exactly zero websites that could help with both. She googled. She scrolled. She opened 47 tabs. She googled again. The right activity was always out there somewhere — just never on the website she was currently rage-refreshing.

After roughly the thousandth "this site has everything except the thing I need," she did what exhausted parents dream about but rarely do: she said "fine, I'll build it myself" — the one website she'd actually want to use every single day. And reader… this is it.

Her husband — official Chief Cheerleader & Tech Support — helped get the whole thing off the ground. What started as one mom's revenge against bad websites is now a small, slightly magical place that makes finding kids' activities feel less like a chore and more like a joy. 🪄

What we believe

📚
Curiosity is a habit

Kids who get exposed to a wide range — math, music, nature, cities — keep wondering as adults. We design for breadth as well as depth.

⏰
Time is the real luxury

Parents don't need another tab open. One curated calendar, one trusted booking flow, real humans answering the phone.

🌱
Small groups, real adults

Every tutor is vetted, CORI-cleared, and chooses to be here. Maximum 12 kids per group. Eye contact still works.

🗺️
Boston is the textbook

Museums, ballet, the Esplanade, Walden Pond — our city is full of free or near-free learning. We help you actually use it.

🤝
Two languages, one family

EN / RU bilingual programs for kids whose parents want both — without making it feel like extra homework.

✨
It should feel like Oz

A little magical. A little theatrical. Kids should leave a GOkidOZ day with a story to tell at dinner.
