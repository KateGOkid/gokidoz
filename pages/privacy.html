Privacy Policy

Plain-English summary of what we collect, why, and how we look after it.

Last updated: May 30, 2026

GOkidOZ Boston ("GOkidOZ," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website and how we use it. By using our site, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Information you give us: when you fill out our contact or sign-up forms, we collect your name, email address, and any details you choose to include in your message.
  • Automatic information: like most websites, we may collect basic technical data (browser type, device, pages visited) through standard analytics to help us improve the site.
  • Ratings & community posts: any rating or comment you submit is stored in your own browser and, where applicable, associated with the content you posted.

2. How we use it

  • To respond to your questions and requests.
  • To send you updates or our newsletter — only if you ask us to. You can unsubscribe anytime.
  • To understand how the site is used so we can make it better.

3. What we don't do

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share it with third parties except as needed to operate the site (for example, our email or hosting providers) or as required by law.

4. Children's privacy

GOkidOZ is a resource for parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

5. Links to other sites

Our directory links out to many third-party providers (camps, classes, tutors, venues). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites — please review their own policies.

6. Cookies & local storage

We use minimal cookies and your browser's local storage to remember preferences (such as ratings you've left). You can clear these anytime through your browser settings.

7. Your choices

You may request a copy of the information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Just email us.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email kate@gokidoz.com.
