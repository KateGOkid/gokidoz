🚲 Biking & Hiking

Family bike trails & easy hikes

Paved, mostly-flat rail trails the whole family can ride — plus where to rent bikes. Everything below is on the map: 🟢 trails, 🔵 bike rentals.

🚲 Trails

Paved & family-friendly. Ride a short section and turn around — no need to do the whole length.

Cape Cod Rail Trail
🚲 Free
⭐ 25+ mi paved, mostly-flat trail · family sections Brewster→Orleans & Nickerson→Orleans · ice cream, lakes & beaches · separated from traffic
📍 Where
South Yarmouth → Wellfleet (Cape Cod)
🎯 Best for
Ages 7–14 · ride a short flat section
🌐 Site
mass.gov ↗
🚲 Trail info ↗
Shining Sea Bikeway
🚲 Free
10.7 mi round-trip · ocean views, salt marshes & Woods Hole harbor · finish at the science museums & ferry watching · mostly flat & paved
📍 Where
Falmouth → Woods Hole (Cape Cod)
🎯 Best for
Ages 7–14 · ocean scenery
📍 Map ↗
Cape Cod Canal Bikeway
🚲 Free
⭐ Best for little kids · 14 mi round-trip · wide, completely flat, no road crossings · constant boat & ship watching along the canal
📍 Where
Bourne & Sandwich (Cape Cod Canal)
🎯 Best for
Ages 4–8 · strollers welcome
🌐 Site
capecodcanal.us ↗
🚲 Canal info ↗
Nickerson State Park — loops & hikes

Easy bike loops & walking trails · small lakes, forests & swimming beaches · great for ages 4–8 & first-time riders · direct Rail Trail access
📍 Where
3488 Main St, Brewster (Cape Cod)
